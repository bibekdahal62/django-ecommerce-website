About NepalCloth

Preserving Tradition, Embracing Modernity

Our Story

Founded in 2023, NepalCloth emerged from a deep passion for Nepali culture and craftsmanship.

We started as a small initiative to promote authentic Nepali clothing and have grown into a trusted platform connecting local artisans with fashion enthusiasts worldwide.

Our journey began in the heart of Kathmandu, where we witnessed the incredible skill of local weavers and artisans creating beautiful traditional garments.

Our Mission

To preserve and promote Nepali textile heritage while providing sustainable livelihood opportunities for local artisans through fair trade practices.

Our Vision

To become the global gateway for authentic Nepali clothing, making traditional craftsmanship accessible to everyone while maintaining cultural integrity.

What We Offer

A diverse collection of authentic Nepali clothing

Traditional Wear

Authentic Daura Suruwal, Dhaka Topi, and traditional women's attire

Modern Fusion

Contemporary designs inspired by traditional Nepali patterns

Handicrafts

Pashmina shawls, Lokta paper products, and traditional accessories
